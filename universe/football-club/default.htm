Objectives

  • To use Open Fun Football Schools as a mechanism that can open up the local football clubs and facilities to the general public

  • To encourage the local football clubs to organise grassroots football for children on a day-to-day basis

Strategy

  • We bring children to the football clubs

  • We bring educated coaches to the football clubs

  • We bring equipment to the local football clubs
