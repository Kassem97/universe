The Senior Leaders Follow-up Education

During the past years we have witnessed a substantial increase in grassroots football activities in all the countries, we are involved in. Correspondingly, the challenge today is to encourage a viable and sustainable local grassroots structure within the framework of the National Football Associations.

The Senior Leaders Follow-up Education is a follow up education that was organised by Cross Cultures in cooperation with the Football Association of Norway and the Danish Sports Federation (DIF). The Follow-up Education is targeting three key persons in the various countries. One is appointed by the National FA and the remaining two by CCPA.

The objective is to provide the key persons with a theoretical and practical knowledge of how to run a project that will help promote building viable and sustainable grassroots football structures.
